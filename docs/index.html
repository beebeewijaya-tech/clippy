Features

Everything you copy, always within reach

Simple by design. Powerful by default. Clippy works silently so you never lose what you copy.

⚡

Automatic Capture

Every text you copy is captured instantly in the background. No shortcuts to learn, no buttons to press.

↩️

One-Click Re-copy

Click any item in your history to instantly copy it back to your clipboard. Get back to work faster.

🗑️

Delete Entries

Remove individual items you no longer need. Keep your history clean and relevant.

💾

Persistent History

Your clips survive app restarts. Up to 150 entries stored locally, old ones trimmed automatically.

📌

Pin Items

Pin your most important clips so they always stay at the top, never lost in the scroll.

🪶

Truly Lightweight

Lives entirely in the menu bar. No Dock icon, no window clutter. Runs invisibly until you need it.

Menu Bar Panel

Everything at a click

Open the panel directly from your menu bar. Your full clipboard history is right there — browse, copy, pin, or delete in seconds.

Clipboard history Pinning Multi-line support URL detection Code snippets
🔒

Your data never leaves your Mac

Clippy requires no accounts, no sign-in, and no internet connection. All clipboard history is stored locally on your device. Nothing is ever sent to any server. Read our Privacy Policy →
